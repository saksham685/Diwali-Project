Role of Hobbies in Mental Health

Sports

Engaging in sports reduces stress, improves mood, and boosts overall mental resilience.

Music

Listening to or playing music enhances relaxation, reduces anxiety, and lifts mood.

Reading

Reading promotes mindfulness, helps escape stress, and enhances empathy and understanding.

Traveling

Traveling broadens perspective, reduces stress, and brings new, refreshing experiences.

Painting

Expressing through painting allows emotional release, reduces anxiety, and boosts creativity.

Gaming

Gaming sharpens problem-solving skills, promotes relaxation, and can enhance social bonds.
